Cyber Security

Learn the fundamentals of cyber security, including threat types, protection strategies, and best practices to safeguard digital assets.

What you'll learn

  • Basics of cyber security and why it matters
  • Common cyber threats and attacks
  • Safe internet and online behavior
  • Password security and authentication basics
  • Malware, phishing, and social engineering awareness
  • Basic network and device security
  • Introduction to ethical hacking concepts
  • Cyber security best practices for daily life

  1. Introduction to Cyber Security
  2. Common Cyber Attacks

Requirements

  • Basic computer knowledge
  • Laptop or desktop
  • Internet connection
  • Willingness to learn independently

Description

The Cyber Security – Beginner Level course is designed for absolute beginners who want to understand how to stay safe in the digital world. This course focuses on awareness, fundamentals, and practical safety practices, not advanced hacking. Learners will gain a strong foundation in cyber security concepts and understand how common attacks work and how to protect themselves and others. This course is ideal for students, job seekers, office users, and anyone interested in starting a career or improving awareness in cyber security.
