Google Workspace

Learn to use Google Workspace tools like Gmail, Google Drive, Docs, Sheets, and Slides for productivity and collaboration in personal and professional settings.

What you'll learn

  • Professional use of Gmail for work
  • Advanced features of Google Docs
  • Intermediate Google Sheets functions and data handling
  • Creating and managing Google Forms
  • Using Google Drive for collaboration and storage
  • Team collaboration with comments, sharing, and permissions
  • Productivity tips and real-world workflows

Course Content

  1. Gmail – Professional Usage
  2. Google Drive – Collaboration & Storage
  3. Google Docs – Intermediate
  4. Google Sheets – Intermedia
  5. Google Forms – Practical Use
  6. Real-World Practice

Requirements

  • Basic computer knowledge
  • Laptop or Desktop
  • Internet Connection for online learning
  • Willingness to practice

Description

The Google Workspace – Intermediate course is designed for learners who already use Google tools and want to improve their productivity for professional and office environments. This course focuses on real-world usage, collaboration, and efficient workflows using Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Forms, and Drive. By the end of the course, learners will be able to manage work emails, collaborate on documents, analyze data in Sheets, and create forms for data collection with confidence. This course is ideal for students, office staff, educators, freelancers, and small business teams.
